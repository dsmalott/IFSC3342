Meet the Artists

Brad

Based in central Arkansas, Brad has been engaged in some form of art for as long as he can remember. From sketching during study hall to singing, playing guitar and writing lyrics for multiple bands, Brad’s passion has always been about creating meaning and experiences through words and imagery. An avid gamer since high school, he’s a natural storyteller and is now a co-founder and designer at Little Rock Games.

Most recently, Brad’s creative interests have moved towards specialized landscape and outdoor photography in The Natural State and beyond. He is passionate about showing the seldom seen, the small and intimate, and the strangely unique beauty of our world. Wherever his travels take him, from the Gulf Coast, through the Rockies, to the Desert Southwest, rest assured Brad’s camera will be at the ready to capture stories through images that are typically left unseen.

Gaylan & Jo

When two artists collide, beautiful things ensue.

Jo and Gaylan are an amazing couple who first met through their involvement with Unity Martial Arts, a community-focused Chung-Nu dojo in central Arkansas. Immediately connected by common interests like fantasy world building, gaming, and anime, their relationship continued to grow even through Jo's travels to Japan to study art with a focus on anime.

Gaylan graduated with a BFA emphasis in furniture design and woodworking. He enjoys using traditional tools and metal forging as well. You can see his rustic iron pieces incorporated into many of his projects. For Jo's trip to Japan, Gaylan gifted her with a custom-made Pai Sho board and pieces that he designed and made with Redoodle.

Jo has numerous interests across multiple mediums and styles. In addition to anime, she paints with water colors, does leatherwork, and recently expanded her portfolio to include laser carving and engraving. An avid tea drinker, Jo has even been known to make her own tea blends for friends and family.
