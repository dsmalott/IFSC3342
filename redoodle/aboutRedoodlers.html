Where Creativity Becomes Reality

Redoodle has been tempting us for many years, and we finally took the leap to make it a reality. I've said for a couple of decades that we weren't using Ragan's artistic skills to their fullest extent. Life finally stopped nudging us about this dream and shoved us into gear. We are taking the Man of a Million Hobbies and letting his artistic and technical talents merge on a larger scale.

But Redoodle isn't just about us. It's about all the dreamers and designers who need an easy way to see their ideas take shape. Whether you want to have a creative drawing carved in beautiful wood, need to prototype an idea buzzing in your head, or need a custom part made that you haven't been able to find, we want to help you realize your dreams and provide tangible results. We offer consulting, design, computer graphics and ultimately the object you've been toying with in your mind. Whether you are in the doodling stage or already have your SVG file ready to carve or print, our team is here to re-doodle your ideas into reality.

We are a family-run business, and we are very focused on inclusion and community. We consider our clients and contributing artists part of our extended family. Redoodle is by nature a creative process, and we want to make it enjoyable and enriching for everyone involved.

A quick note about Intellectual Property (IP) and Open Source - we work with an honor system regarding IP, and we also encourage and contribute to Open Source projects. We will never “steal your idea” for profit or share anything about your project without your explicit permission. We trust our clients, partners and artists to do the same in return. Our contributions to Open Source communities are based on our own designs and enhancements we make, or stumble upon, for the Maker Community.
